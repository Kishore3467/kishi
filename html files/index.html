UG Courses

The full form for UG is undergraduate. Undergraduate refers to the studies done after completing schooling (post-secondary education) and before attaining a postgraduate degree. The idea to pursue a UG course is one of the most crucial and challenging phases in any student's life..

BA Tamil

The core BA Tamil subjects are Modern Literature, English, Journalism, Environmental Education, and Tamil. Graduates with a BA in Tamil can improve their knowledge, comprehension, listening, and writing skills in Tamil. BA Tamil students study linguistics from the perspectives of grammar and literature

BA English

BA English course deals with the study of English Literature, history of English literature, cultural studies, modern literature and more. As part of BA English course, students are taught about various topics such as poetry, dramas, Indian Classical Literature, Women's Writing, Literature and Caste and more.

BBA

BBA is a 3-year degree course that is divided into 6 semesters. You will learn a wide variety of subjects. You will also get an opportunity to choose specialisations. Your final grade and percentage will be an average of all the 3 years of the course.

B.Com

B.Com is an undergraduate academic degree awarded for a course or program in Commerce. The duration of the B.Com course is 3 years in India, divided into six distinct semesters. The B.Com courses list includes Marketing, accounting, entrepreneurship, various taxation and industrial laws etc.

B.Com (CA)

Chartered Accountancy is known to be a challenging course which delves into both conceptual understanding and extensive practical knowledge. BCom with CA is divided into six semesters, and various topics are present in the BCom Syllabus to increase the skill threshold of the student.

B.Sc Physical Education

BSc Physical Education is the study of body development, strength, physical coordination, and agility is known as physical education. This course's curriculum typically includes Physical fitness, health-related physical fitness, and performance-related physical fitness are all terms used to describe physical fitness.

B.Sc Mathematics

BSc Mathematics is a 3-year UG course typically offered by universities and colleges and is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in mathematical theory, techniques, and applications

B.Sc Physics

BSc Physics or Bachelor of Science in Physics is a three-year full-time bachelor's degree programme which aims to teach students the fundamentals of physics and other topics revolving around it. BSc Physics subjects are electromagnetism, quantum mechanics, waves, calculus, semiconductors, optics, and statistics.

B.Sc Chemistry

BSc Chemistry degree is generally of a duration of 3 to 4 years, which is divided into several semesters. What are the core subjects taught in the BSc Chemistry syllabus? The core subjects taught in the BSc Chemistry degree include Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry and Analytical Chemistry.

B.Sc Computer Science

BSc Computer Science is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of computer systems and software development which is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in Computer Science principles, as well as practical skills in programming, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering.

PG Courses

The minimum eligibility for getting into post-graduate courses is after completion of a bachelor's degree. Some postgraduate programs are subject-specific. One has to pursue a particular subject or subject in their undergraduate program and score certain cut-off marks to apply for a particular postgraduate course.

Master of Commerce

MCom is a two-year postgraduate course usually taken up by students after completing BCom. MCom admission 2024 is generally through an entrance exam, usually held at university-level, or based on merit. MCom degrees are most commonly taken up by students who want to become teachers/lecturers and researchers

Master of Mathematics

An MSc in Mathematics, or Master of Science in Mathematics, is a 2-year postgraduate academic degree that allows students to deepen their understanding and expertise in various areas of mathematics. MSc Mathematics typically requires students to have a strong foundation in mathematics from their undergraduate studies.

Master of English

MA English is a two-year postgraduate programme that focuses on the study of English Literature and Language throughout the world. The MA English course provides students with the analytical and critical thinking skills necessary to generate original insights in scholarly academic writing and discourse.
